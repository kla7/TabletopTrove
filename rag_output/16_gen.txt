Recommendation: One Page Fantasy

Description: One Page Fantasy is a strategy battle game designed to be played with WHFB miniatures. The game offers simple mechanics based around alternating phases, allowing you to play highly tactical games of regimental warfare. Players draft teams of 4 heroes with individual sets of skills and try to beat the crap out of each other. The game is set in a mixed science/fantasy setting, with both metal robots and dragons. Depth of game play is claimed to be one of its features. The game is easy to learn and requires minimal setup, making it a great option for new players or casual gamers. The game is also highly replayable, with different scenarios and heroes to try out. Overall, One Page Fantasy is a fun and challenging game that is perfect for fans of strategy and tabletop gaming.
