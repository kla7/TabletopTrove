Kaiju Rampage: Invasion of Gamera Supplement

Kaiju Rampage: Invasion of Gamera Supplement is an expansion for the base game, Godzilla Rampage. It includes new and updated Kaiju cards, 2 new scenarios, and special ability cards for Kaiju. The expansion supports 2-12 players and has a typical playtime of 30-45 minutes. The new Kaiju cards introduce new monsters, such as Gamera, while the new scenarios offer fresh challenges for players to overcome. The special ability cards provide additional strategies and tactics for players to employ in their battles. Overall, Kaiju Rampage: Invasion of Gamera Supplement is a great addition to the Godzilla Rampage board game, offering new gameplay mechanics and exciting challenges for players to enjoy.
