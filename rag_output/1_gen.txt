Word Wars is a word game for two players. Players start at opposite ends of the board and interlock words to compete in a race to cross the board. By interlocking your chain of words with that of your opponents, you can capture or remove some of their letters from the board and gain an advantage. The game includes a board, tile holders, and 150 letter tiles. Typical playtime is 60 minutes. Recommended for ages 8 and up. Average rating: Average rated game (5.7/10.0).
