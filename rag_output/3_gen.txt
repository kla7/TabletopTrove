Space Explorer: This game is a highly rated cooperative game where players take turns controlling a space ship going from planet to planet. The game is a race against time measured in deck reshuffles. Players use a deck of cards to determine distance and direction, and the game is a race against time. The game is highly rated and recommended for ages 12 and up. Average rating: Highly rated game (7.3/10.0). Players work together to achieve their goal, but players can also bluff about what they are playing and use special abilities to switch the positions of the blueprints, use agent cards to reveal an opponent's cards, block an opponent's progress, and more. The game is a fun and challenging cooperative game that is suitable for players of all ages.
