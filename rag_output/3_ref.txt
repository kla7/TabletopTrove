**Space Explorer**: It falls under the following categories: Science Fiction. It has the following mechanics: Co-operative Play, Hand Management, Line Drawing, Paper-and-Pencil, Point to Point Movement. It supports 2-4 players. Recommended for ages 12 and up. Average rating: Highly rated game (7.3/10.0). Described in the book Beyond Competition, Space Explorer is a cooperative game where players take turns controlling a space ship going from planet to planet.  Distance and direction is determined by a deck of cards (1-6 in four suits).  The game is a race against time measured in deck reshuffles.
