**Spice Merchants**: It falls under the following categories: Economic, Travel. It has the following mechanics: Commodity Speculation, Pick-up and Deliver, Roll / Spin and Move. It supports 2-6 players. A game of travel and trade set in the late 1500's and early 1600's. Each player is an independent European merchant traveling along the trade routes between Venice, Italy and the Far East. Players buy various spices and commodities in the hopes of a large pay-off when delivered in Venice.
