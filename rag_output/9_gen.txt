Recommendation: Don't be Greedy

Description: Don't be Greedy is a press-your-luck game for kids by Melissa and Doug. Players take turns drawing jewels from a bag to put in their treasure chests. If they draw a frown, they have to put their treasure back in the bag. The game is simple to learn, but the luck of the draw can make it challenging. Players must balance their risk-taking with their need to collect jewels to fill their treasure chests. The game is suitable for kids aged 5 and up, and the average rating is 8.0/10.0. The game includes 4 jewels, a bag, and a set of instructions. The game is a great way to teach kids about the importance of risk-taking and the value of collecting and storing treasures.
